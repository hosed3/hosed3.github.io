our services

Domestic Building

Domestic building services help homeowners with building, renovating, or improving their residential properties. This can include everything from design and planning to construction and renovation.

Gardens

Garden services are designed to help homeowners maintain and enhance their outdoor spaces, such as gardens, lawns, and landscapes. Services can include lawn mowing, hedge trimming, planting, or designing a new outdoor space.

Tiling

Tiling services help homeowners and businesses with tile installation, repair, and maintenance. This can include selecting the right tiles, cutting and laying them to create a finished surface.

Painting

Painting services help homeowners and businesses with painting and decorating. This includes selecting colors and finishes, preparing surfaces, and applying paint.

Home Insulation

Home insulation services help homeowners improve the energy efficiency of their homes. This can include assessing current insulation levels, installing new insulation materials, and reducing energy consumption to lower utility bills.

Full House Renovation

Full house renovation services assist homeowners in completely remodeling and updating their homes, including planning, design, demolition, construction, and finishing work.

Loft Conversions

Loft conversions are a construction service that transforms underutilized attic space into functional living areas like an extra bedroom, office, or entertainment room. This involves designing, planning, reinforcing the structure, installing insulation, flooring, walls, windows, electrical, and plumbing systems. It's a cost-effective way to add valuable space and value to a home without the need for costly extensions or relocation.

Extensions

House extensions are a construction service that adds new rooms or enlarges existing ones, expanding residential properties. This involves designing, planning, reinforcing the structure, installing insulation, roofing, flooring, walls, windows, electrical, and plumbing systems. House extensions provide additional living space like a larger kitchen, living room, or bedroom, and increase the property's value and functionality.
